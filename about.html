About Me

I am currently pursuing my Master of Library and Information Science degree with a concentration on School Library Media at Rutgers, the State University of New Jersey. My professional interests include information literacy, the information behavior of urban adolescents, YA literature, and classroom technology integration.

I earned my B.A. in English Literature at CUNY Hunter College, where I was a Macaulay Honors College scholar. I spent the year following my graduation working as a Database Research Assistant for Entertainment Weekly's EW.com, where I worked on the online magazine archive, and occasionally wrote for the website. Currently, I am Assistant to the Directors of Admissions and College Guidance at Lawrence Woodmere Academy in Woodmere, NY.

In my spare time I enjoy reading — my favorite authors are Jane Austen, Ian McEwan, Edith Wharton, and Elizabeth Gaskell — watching movies, baking, and spoiling my pet rabbit, Bobbie.
